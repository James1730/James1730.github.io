About Sundays

Where Craftsmanship Meets Precision

A Legacy of Billiard Excellence

Founded in 2005, Sundays (SDAiS) has grown into one of Guangzhou's leading billiard table manufacturers, serving clubs, distributors, and sports venues across 50+ countries.

Our 8,000 m² factory combines traditional woodworking craftsmanship with modern CNC precision. Every table features hand-selected solid hardwood, Italian slate beds, and championship-grade cloth.

From American pool tables to full-size snooker tables, coin-operated commercial units to bespoke luxury designs — we deliver complete solutions including accessories and branded packaging.
