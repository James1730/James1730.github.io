The Future of Commercial Billiard Room Design: Trends for 2026-2027

📅 April 18, 2026 • ✍️ Sundays Editorial • ⏱ 12 min read

The traditional, dimly lit pool hall is a thing of the past. Today's commercial billiard venues are evolving into high-end lifestyle lounges that blend gaming, hospitality, and cutting-edge technology. Based on our global distribution network, here are the top design trends for 2026-2027.

1. Integrated LED Lighting

Lighting is no longer just a overhead box. Modern clubs are integrating LED strips into the table frame, the legs, and even the room's architecture. Smart lighting systems that change color based on the game status or the venue's mood are becoming a standard requirement for premium clubs.

2. Smart Scoreboards and Analytics

Distributors are increasingly asking for tables that can be integrated with digital scoring systems. Whether it's overhead cameras for game replay or integrated sensors in the pockets, technology is bringing the professional tournament experience to every local club.

3. The "Lifestyle Lounge" Concept

Modern venues are moving away from rows of tables and towards "private gaming pods." These spaces include premium seating, dedicated service areas, and high-end billiard tables that serve as the centerpiece of a social experience.
