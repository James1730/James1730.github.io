Sustainable Billiard Manufacturing: Our Commitment to Quality and Environment

📅 April 10, 2026 • ✍️ Sundays Editorial • ⏱ 8 min read

Sustainability is no longer just a buzzword; it's a responsibility. At Sundays Billiard, we've integrated eco-friendly practices into our 8,000m² manufacturing facility in Guangzhou. We believe that producing the world's finest billiard tables shouldn't come at the cost of the environment.

1. Responsibly Sourced Hardwoods

We exclusively use hardwoods sourced from sustainably managed forests. Whether it's the solid maple used in our competition pool tables or the mahogany for our luxury series, every piece of timber is traceable and harvested in compliance with international environmental standards.

2. Minimizing Waste in Production

Through advanced CNC machining and optimized cutting patterns, we've reduced timber waste by 25% over the last five years. Any wood scrap produced is repurposed or recycled, ensuring that very little material ends up in landfills.

3. Eco-Friendly Finishes and Adhesives

The paints, stains, and glues used in our tables are low-VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds), making them safer for our workers and better for the indoor air quality of your club or home.
