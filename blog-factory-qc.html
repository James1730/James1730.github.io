The Gold Standard: Sundays' 12-Step Quality Control Process

📅 July 10, 2026 • ✍️ Factory Management • ⏱ 10 min read

Consistency is what builds a brand. At Sundays Billiard, our 8,000m² factory operates under a rigorous 12-step quality control system. Every table that leaves our facility in Guangzhou is a testament to our commitment to craftsmanship.

1. Raw Material Inspection

Our QC team begins at the source, inspecting every shipment of solid hardwood for moisture content and structural integrity. Only timber that meets our strict density requirements is allowed into production.

2. Precision CNC Milling

Using state-of-the-art CNC machinery, we ensure that every pocket, leg, and frame component is cut with millimeter precision. This automated accuracy eliminates human error during the assembly phase.

3. Pre-Assembly & Leveling

Before packing, every table is fully pre-assembled in our showroom. We conduct a "ball-rolling test" and use laser-leveling equipment to verify the flatness of the slate bed. This ensures that the table you receive is ready for professional play.
